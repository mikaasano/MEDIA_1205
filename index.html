My Services

At Mika Asano Marketing, I offer a range of services to elevate your brand's online presence and drive results. From strategic planning to execution, I work closely with each client to deliver campaigns that resonate with their target audience. One of my esteemed clients, Kosmo Technologies, has entrusted me with their marketing needs, and together, we've achieved remarkable success. Let's collaborate to achieve similar outcomes for your business!

Here is a list of services you can expect when you work with me:

  1. Brand Goals, KPIs, and Measurement Definitions: Before diving into any campaign, it's crucial to align on your brand's goals and key performance indicators (KPIs). I'll work closely with you to define these metrics and establish clear measurement frameworks to track success effectively.
  2. Creative Recommendations: Creativity is at the heart of every successful marketing campaign. I'll provide innovative and impactful creative recommendations tailored to your brand's voice and objectives, ensuring your message resonates with your target audience.
  3. Target Audience Identification: Understanding your audience is essential for crafting compelling marketing strategies. Using advanced analytics and audience insights, I'll help you identify and segment your target audience, allowing for more precise targeting and messaging.
  4. Full-Funnel Tactical Marketing Strategy: From awareness to conversion, I'll develop a full-funnel marketing strategy that guides consumers seamlessly through each stage of the buyer's journey. Whether you're looking to drive brand awareness or boost sales, I'll implement tactics designed to achieve your objectives at every stage.
  5. Campaigns Running Cross-Platforms: Reach your audience wherever they are with campaigns running across various platforms. From social media platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn to search engines like Google, as well as programmatic DSPs (Demand-Side Platforms), I'll ensure your message reaches the right people at the right time.
  6. Campaign Monitoring and Real-Time Optimizations: Continuous monitoring is key to campaign success. I'll closely monitor campaign performance in real-time, making data-driven optimizations to maximize ROI and ensure your campaigns are delivering optimal results.
  7. Post Reporting with Data, Insights, and Future Recommendations: After the campaign concludes, I'll provide comprehensive post-reporting, including detailed data analysis, actionable insights, and future recommendations. This valuable information will inform future strategies and ensure ongoing success.
